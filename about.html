About Us

The Job Corner (TJC)

Welcome to The Job Corner (TJC) – a simple, free, and powerful platform created to help small MSMEs connect with job seekers quickly and easily.

Our mission is to make hiring for small-scale jobs effortless, without the cost and complexity of big recruitment portals.

Whether you’re a small shop owner looking for part-time help or a skilled professional searching for opportunities in your city, TJC bridges the gap — all at zero cost.

This project was proudly built by Advik Pal, an IIM Bangalore BBA DBE student, during a hackathon. The idea was simple: create something useful, accessible, and impactful for our local communities.
“Empowering small businesses, one job at a time.”
